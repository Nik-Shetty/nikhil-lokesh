// Origin Story

The Journey

Every great programme manager is forged on a factory floor. Mine started on the shop floor of one of India's premier Tier-1 aerospace suppliers — where I learned that understanding what you manage is the only real advantage.

2021 — THE FOUNDATION

Born on the Shop Floor

Stepped onto the Rangsons Aerospace factory floor as a Graduate Engineer Trainee. Fuselage structures, aerodefence assemblies, AS9100 tolerances. I didn't just observe — I absorbed. Every rivet, every weld, every inspection taught me what a programme manager must never lose sight of: the physics of the promise.

01
2022 — SYSTEMS THINKING

Enterprise Scale at DXC

A deliberate pivot into global enterprise IT operations, supporting Honeywell users across four continents. Here I built the discipline of structured problem-solving, escalation protocols, and documentation rigour — the invisible backbone of every programme I've managed since.

02
2023 — FIRST PROGRAMME CHAIR

Building the Function from Zero

Rangsons had no programme management function for structural assembly. I built it. From first principles. Seven OEM customers. A USD 4M portfolio. 130% revenue growth. And every lesson was earned the hard way — late nights with APQP plans, customer escalations at dawn, and supplier negotiations that taught me what leverage really means.

03
2025 — THE APEX

Executive Programme Manager, TASL

Tata Advanced Systems. Boeing 787 Dreamliner. The most iconic widebody aircraft in the sky. I own this programme end-to-end — composites production, OEM negotiations, board-level reporting, P&L. The factory floor taught me what matters. Everything since has been preparation for this chair.
